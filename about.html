About Plain5

Simplifying complex documents for everyone.

Our Story

Plain5 was born from a simple truth: complex documents create unnecessary stress. Whether it's insurance policies, medical bills, or critical notices, we've all struggled to make sense of dense, jargon-filled text.

We built Plain5 to change that — combining AI-powered clarity with the trust of a secure personal vault. Our mission is to help people not only understand their important documents but also keep them safe, organized, and ready for action.

At Plain5, we believe clarity and security are rights, not privileges. We're here to put that power in your hands.

Our Mission

Plain5 exists to give people clarity, control, and confidence over their most critical documents.

Our mission is to transform static files into secure, action-ready experiences—combining AI-powered insights, task extraction, and draft assistance, all inside a private, personal vault.

We aim to make document clarity and smart decision-making accessible to everyone, turning paperwork from a source of stress into a tool for action.

Our Values

Simplicity

We believe in making complex things simple. Our technology breaks down complicated documents into clear, actionable insights.

Privacy

Your documents are private. We use state-of-the-art encryption and security measures to protect your data.

Trust

We build trust through transparency. Our AI-powered insights are backed by human expertise and rigorous testing.
